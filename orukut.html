Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: Vegetable-Burger
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 pieces
Tomato 4 sliced pieces
onions 4 sliced pieces
Mayonaisse 3 table spoons

Step 1: use a table knife to slip your mayonaisse over the bread

Step 2: put out your two pieces of bread

Step 3: place your tomato over your bread

Step 4: place your onions over your tomato

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: Fried-eggs
Ingredients Quantity
Oil Enough Oil to prepare 4 eggs
Eggs 4 pieces
maggi 2 pieces
Pepper 2 table-spoons
Onions 2 pieces of onions

Step 1:pour your oil into the frying pan

Step 2: crack your egg into a bowl

Step 3: add your maggi, pepper and onions into the cracked eggs

Step 4:mix your egg properly

Step 5:pour your eggs into the frying pan and fry till your liking.
